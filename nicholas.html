Projects

Noodle.com

Created by our team during the WDCC x SESA Hackathon. The theme was "facilitating procrastination".
Whenever the user tries to use a productivity related website such as Wikipedia, they are redirected by a Chrome extension to Noodle.com. Noodle.com is a search engine where all the search results are useless things such as gifs, puns, and videos. Users are able to submit their own content to be added into our database as future search results.
